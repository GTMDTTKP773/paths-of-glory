Paths of Glory : Implementation notes

Paths of Glory has a few abreviations that are present in the rules and log of this game.

  • AH: Austro-Hungarian
  • ANA: Arab Northern Army
  • AoI: Army of Islam
  • AUS: Australian
  • BE: Belgian
  • BEF: British Expeditionary Force
  • BR: British
  • BU: Bulgarian
  • CAU: Caucasus
  • CND: Canadian
  • CP: Central Powers
  • FR: French
  • GE: German
  • GR: Greek
  • IT: Italian
  • MEF: Mediterranean Expeditionary Force
  • MN: Montenegrin
  • NE: Near East
  • PT: Portuguese
  • RO: Romania
  • RU: Russian
  • SB: Serbian
  • SN: Senussi Tribal
  • TU: Turkish
  • US: United States
  • YLD: Yilderim (Lightning)

The log is following the Paths Of Glory Vassal modules conventions.

  • Reduced armies or corps are shown between brackets, e.g (FR 2)
  • When a corps enters or leaves the Reserve box for any reason, the number of corps in the reserve is shown between brackets
  • For example "(FR 5) in Sedan breaks to FRc(6,0)" means that a french reduced army has been broken down to a corps, taken from the Reserve box which now contains 6 full-strength corps and 0 reduced corps of that nationality."

Is there a way to supply warn my enemy ?

Yes. Click on the oil barrel at the top of the screen, then "Flag Supply warnings" Once you click on Done, your opponent will be able to see which spaces you have flagged.

Is there a way to Rollback ?

You can rollback by clicking on the oil barrel at the top of the screen, then "Propose Rollback". It will show you the rollback options available and wil show you the Die roll cancelled by that rollback. Your opponent will have to accept the rollback for it to be effective.
